# National University of Singapore (NUS)

The **National University of Singapore** (NUS) is a national research university in Singapore. Founded in 1905 as the Straits Settlements and Federated Malay States Government Medical School, it is the oldest and most prestigious autonomous university in Singapore.

## Key Facts
- **Location**: Kent Ridge, Singapore (Main Campus).
- **Motto**: "Towards a Global Knowledge Enterprise".
- **Reputation**: Consistently ranked as one of the top universities in Asia and the world. It is known for its high-impact research and global outlook.

## Academics
NUS offers a broad-based curriculum underscored by multi-disciplinary courses and cross-faculty enrichment. Key faculties include:
- Faculty of Engineering
- Faculty of Science
- NUS Business School
- Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine
- School of Computing
- Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy

## Research & Innovation
NUS focuses on key research areas such as:
- Biomedical Sciences
- Environmental and Water Technologies
- Materials Science
- Smart Nation (AI, Data Science)

The university has a strong entrepreneurial culture, with "Block71" being a famous startup hub associated with NUS Enterprise.
