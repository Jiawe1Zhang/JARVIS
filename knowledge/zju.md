# Zhejiang University (ZJU)

**Zhejiang University** (ZJU) is a comprehensive research university located in Hangzhou, the capital of Zhejiang Province, China. Founded in 1897 as Qiushi Academy, it is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher education in China.

## Key Facts
- **Location**: Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China.
- **Motto**: "Seeking Truth, Pursuing Innovation" (求是创新).
- **Reputation**: Often ranked among the top universities in China (C9 League) and globally. It is renowned for its engineering, computer science, and agricultural programs.

## Campus
ZJU has seven campuses, with the Zijingang Campus being the main one. The campuses are known for their beautiful scenery, blending modern architecture with traditional Chinese elements.

## Academics
The university offers a wide range of disciplines, including:
- Engineering
- Medicine
- Agriculture
- Arts and Humanities
- Social Sciences
- Science
- Information Technology

ZJU is a major center for research and innovation in China, contributing significantly to the country's technological and scientific advancements.
